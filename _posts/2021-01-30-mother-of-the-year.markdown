The fact that I went out of my way to buy and hem a dress and shoes for her to wear to it, had to drive her there, she refused to wear makeup or let me do her nails or eyebrows and then she refused even this and with an attitude (she told me no thanks at first but when I kept pushing she raised her voice at me and told me to stay out of it!) infuriated me. She is a troublemaker in general and has been causing trouble and treating me with disrespect for years now but this was the straw that broke the camel’s back. 

I went off on her, voicing that I wished that this comes back to bite her in the ass and her daughter treats her the same way she treats me but that I knew it wouldn’t because she would never have a daughter, she would have a miserable life because she treats everyone like sh*t. I also said that she could be such a bitch sometimes (because she can) and then continued with this saying “every other daughter gives their mother something on this day but no, not you!, you only care about yourself don’t you!, you get everything your way around here and you can’t even give me this!, it’s all about you!” and then berated her for all of the ways she has been misbehaving lately (not eating dinner, staying in her room all day, being snappy with me etc.) and told her that she can either stop these behaviours or go live out on the streets cause I don’t give a fuck either way anymore and told her that I “fucking hate” her. 

I obviously don’t really hate her or want her to live out on the streets but I was furious at her and I am feeling resentful towards and frustrated with her. I called my husband about this situation afterwards and I was admittedly very upset so I’m guessing he must have convinced her to let me straighten her hair because she eventually apologised to me for not allowing me to and let me straighten it. 

I apologised to her for getting mad at her and told her that I didn’t hate her or want her to move out but that I  meant what I said about her cleaning up her act so to speak because it is really pushing my buttons. I shouldn’t have said that I hated her or wanted her to move out but I don’t regret getting angry at her as she did deserve it. However, my husband says that I shouldn’t have gotten so mad. So, I’m asking, AITA here?
